## Why a blog (again)

After being on pause for a long while — through a master's, a PhD start, and a lot of conference deadlines — this blog is back. It now lives inside my [academic homepage](/), so everything you see here shares one place, one identity, and one URL.

A few things I want to use it for:

- **Reading notes** for papers I find interesting (especially around computer-using agents, code intelligence, and reasoning).
- **Tooling and infra notes** that are too long for a tweet but too short for a paper.
- **Personal updates** from PhD life, talks, and travels.

## What changed under the hood

The site is now built with Jekyll. Posts go through `_posts/`, the listing page is at `/blog/`, with category and tag pages at `/blog/categories/` and `/blog/tags/`. Older Hexo posts have been migrated and live next to the new ones.

If a post has both English and Chinese versions (like this one), the **English / 中文** toggle near the top will switch between them. Your choice is remembered across the site.

## 为什么又开始写博客

中间停了挺久——从硕士到博士开学，再到各种会议 ddl 把人填得满满的。现在博客回来了，它直接长在我的[学术主页](/)里，所有内容、身份、URL 都集中到一处，不再有"主页是主页、博客是博客"的割裂。

打算写些什么：

- **论文阅读笔记**：尤其是 computer-using agent、代码智能、复杂推理等方向。
- **工具与基础设施备忘**：那种 280 字写不完、写论文又太轻量的内容。
- **PhD 生活的随笔**：旅行、talk、踩坑日记。

## 技术上变了什么

现在整套站点用 Jekyll 构建。文章放在 `_posts/`，列表在 `/blog/`，分类和标签页面分别在 `/blog/categories/` 与 `/blog/tags/`。之前的 Hexo 老文章都迁移过来了，和新文章排在一起。

如果一篇文章同时有中英文版本（比如这一篇），顶部那个 **English / 中文** 按钮就可以切换语言。你的选择会在整站记住。
